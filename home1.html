Aims

ACT’s vision is of a world where pet owners and vets have access to reliable and up to date information so that an early and accurate diagnosis can be made and where up to date information is available on effective cancer treatments and prognosis.

Our Vision

ACT’s vision is of a world where pet owners and vets have access to reliable and up to date information so that an early and accurate diagnosis can be made and where up to date information is available on effective cancer treatments and prognosis.

Our Mission

Our goal is to fulfill our vision by carrying out our objectives to the best of our ability. Our aims are to: Provide education and information that help owners, the veterinary profession and all who care for our pets understand more about cancer and what treatments are possible.
